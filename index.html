About the Creator

18-year old, Kristina Tran, was born in Escondido, California. Ever since she was small, she has been participating in all mediums of music including choirs, orchestras, musicals, rock bands, and solo competitions.

Music has truly shaped her life and she is determined to become successful in the music field. Currently, she is attending the University of California, San Diego to pursue a degree in Interdisciplinary Computing and the Arts with a Focus in Music with goals to hone her skills to later pursue a career as an independent artist/content creator.
